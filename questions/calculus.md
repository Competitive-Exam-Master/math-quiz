# Calculus Questions

## CalQ1

## CalQ2
Evaluate the definite integral:
$$
\int_0^1 (x^2 + 2x) \,dx
$$

## CalQ3

## CalQ4

## CalQ5
Determine the critical points of $h(x) = x^3 - 6x^2 + 9x$.

## CalQ6

## CalQ7

## CalQ8
